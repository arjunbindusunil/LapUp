# Lap Up

**Your field, distilled into a ~25-minute audio brief — every day, made for you.**

Lap Up keeps senior builders on the frontier of their field without the endless tabs, newsletters, and "I'll read it later" pile. Each day it hands you a **lap**: a short, sharp, *spoken* brief on what's genuinely new and worth your attention — pitched at your level, on the things you actually work on. Press play on the commute, the walk, the lift. Come out current.

## What you get
- **A daily lap, made for you.** Not a generic feed — a brief shaped by who you are, what you're building, and what you already know cold (so it skips the basics).
- **Quick Learn.** Want a lap on something specific right now? Ask for it — any topic, your chosen length.
- **A living knowledge map.** Everything you learn forms a glowing constellation of your expertise, with the frontier — what's worth learning next — lit up. Watch it grow as you listen.
- **Peers.** Find people in your field by email, see exactly where your knowledge overlaps, and share a lap straight into a peer's library.
- **Your way to listen.** Story-driven, two-person podcast, or a focused deep dive.

## How it works
1. **Sign in** with Apple or Google.
2. **Tell it about you** — paste a profile, or let an AI you already use describe you.
3. **Watch your map form** and your first laps land.
4. **Listen daily.** Mark what's great, skip what's not — and Lap Up learns.

## Who it's for
Engineers, founders, researchers, and operators who'd rather *understand* the new thing than just bookmark it.
